Ingredients:
 - 1 cup pecans
 - 1 cup dates
 - 5 Tbsp raw cocoa powder
 - 4 Tbsp coconut oil
 - 2 Tbsp raw honey

Directions:
 1. Blend pecans in a food processor until they become powder-like.
 
 2. Add dates to the food processor and blend well.
 
 3. Add remaining ingredients to the food processor and blend until mixture becomes smooth.

 4. Press mixture into a 5 in by 9 in baking pan and flatten out the top.

 5. Place in refrigerator for at least an hour before serving.
